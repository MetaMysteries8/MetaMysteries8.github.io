## Takeaways for play
- Even full 70/120-star runs are long; you only need a few early stars to progress.
- Most individual stars are reachable in well under a minute by going straight to
  the objective — so pick the obvious target and head right for it.
- Speed comes from sustained running and clean lines, not fancy tricks.
